# Chronicles

The **Chronicles** are a historical record of crushr’s evolution.

These are not formal specifications, and they are not canonical documentation.

They are snapshots of:

- what was being explored
- what decisions were made
- how the system changed over time
- and why certain directions were taken

Most entries originated as public writing and have been preserved here as part of the project’s development trail.

## What this section is

- A timeline of major milestones
- A record of design thinking in motion
- Context for how the current system came to be

## What this section is not

- A source of truth for current behavior
- A replacement for documentation or architecture specs
- A guarantee that ideas here are still valid

For current behavior and guarantees, see the formal docs and project control documents.

## Reading guide

- Start with the most recent entries and work backwards
- Treat each entry as a point-in-time perspective
- Expect ideas to evolve or be replaced

Most projects only show their final state.

crushr keeps the path.

Because how a system evolves is often more informative than where it ends up.
