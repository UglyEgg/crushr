## Startup checklist

Read in order:

1. `AGENTS.md`

3. `PROJECT_STATE.md`
4. `.ai/INDEX.md`
5. `.ai/STATUS.md`

Then confirm active phase/step from `.ai/STATUS.md` before coding.
